The Best of

BASIS

Welcome to the 2023–24 issue of Inputs & Outcomes! Published mere months after our network of exceptional public charter schools celebrated its 25th anniversary, we are also tipping our (10-gallon) hats to our 10th anniversary in the state of Texas.

This issue of Inputs & Outcomes presents the best of BASIS.

The best of BASIS is, simply, consistent excellence. Our best isn’t a one-off. Rather, ‘the best of BASIS’ means that we do in every academic year what others may hope to achieve one time, someday. Our best is the best—this year, last year, next year, and every year.

The best of BASIS means education from the best educators in America, utilizing the best curriculum in the country. That’s why our incredible students and supportive families come to BASIS

The best of BASIS is innovation and creativity, plus enthusiasm and dedication—not from one place or source, but from many smart people in many places, with a shared passion to serve as a conduit to the best public education in America.

The best of BASIS means exceptional curricular and pedagogical inputs, resulting in excellent student outcomes.

That’s why we are here: all of us.

“The best of BASIS” may seem familiar, customary, and recognizable. We sure hope so.

That’s precisely the point.

Our Mission

The mission of BASIS Charter Schools is to empower students to achieve at globally competitive levels with our transformative K-12 academic program.

Our Philosophy

Since 1998, BASIS has hired exceptional, expert educators to teach a rigorous academic program to eager students, and achieved exceptional results by doing so, whether assessed campus by campus, or student by student. Our teachers have seen firsthand for more than a quarter century that if you raise the standards of student learning, any student can rise to meet those standards, and exceed them.

The classroom is key. Ours are energized by prioritizing demonstration of deep learning by utilizing projects, presentations, and comprehensive assessments to gauge each student’s intellectual journey. Through hands-on activities and collaborative projects, we ignite critical thinking and foster a love for learning that even extends far beyond the classroom.

All told, we offer students timeless principles: dedication to hard work; embracing accountability; perseverance in the face of challenges; and recognizing your education as the ladder to achieving your aspirations. Our students therefore become participants, not spectators, in the competitive global landscape.
